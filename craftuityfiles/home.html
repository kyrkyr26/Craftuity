Who We Are:

Welcome to Craftuity!
We are a Panamanian brother and sister creative duo based in New Hampshire.
Our names are Kyrsten and Chris.
If you'd like to follow our journey please feel free to follow us on our socials!
We are @Craftuity on Instagram and TikTok!

What We Do:

Don't be fooled by our variety of products, we make everything by hand!
We got resin 3D printers going throughout the night.
Tinkercad, Blender, and Nomad are our best friends for designing and modeling our 3D printed products.
Kyrsten is our resident crocheter, sculptor, and drawer. All crochet, polymer clay, and drawn pieces are done by her.
As you can tell we have a hard time sticking to one thing XD.
